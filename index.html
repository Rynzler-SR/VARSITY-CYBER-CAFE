Company History

Empowering the next generation of gamers since 2023

Our Journey

2023

Founding and Origin

Varsity was found in the 2023 by four information management students who identified a significant gap and problem in the local digital landscape. While traditional cyber cafés focused on primarily on casual gaming, the students noticed that university students and freelancers lacked a dedicated “third place” that combine high-performance gaming with a professional-grade creative environment. By choosing a medium-sized business model, Varsity was able to provide a hub for both competitive e-sports and students. The company quickly grew into the go-to destination for students and enthusiasts alike, providing a modern, comfortable, and high-performance environment for all things digital.

2025

Infrastructure Pivot

We have decided to expand our business in terms of facilities in order to provide a much better and comfortable services to our customers. We were also planning on expanding our business around the globe.
